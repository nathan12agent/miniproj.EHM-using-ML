## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Database Performance**: Implement indexing and query optimization
- **ML Model Accuracy**: Use multiple models and ensemble methods
- **Integration Issues**: Regular testing and modular development
- **Scalability**: Design with scalability in mind from the start

### Timeline Risks
- **Feature Creep**: Stick to defined scope and requirements
- **Technical Challenges**: Allocate buffer time for complex features
- **Team Coordination**: Regular meetings and progress tracking
- **External Dependencies**: Have backup plans for third-party services
